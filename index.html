About Me

My name is Agung Puji Raharjo. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science degree in Robotics at the University of Manchester. My studies at Gadjah Mada University, with a major in Electronics and Instrumentation, have equipped me with the necessary skills and experiences to effectively navigate professional challenges. This portfolio showcases some of my preferred projects and the skills I've acquired.

Projects

Humanoid Dancing Robot

This is one of the things that happened to me during my undergraduate studies. I've been on a robotic team for about three years, and it's helped me develop both my soft skills and hard skills. I started out as an electronic staff member, and my job was to develop PCBs that met the system's requirements. As a team, we used a Teensy microcontroller, a servo motor, and Bluetooth modules to build the robots and establish communication between two robots. I also installed artistic devices and LED to make the performance more attractive. I also lent a hand in creating the robot's costumes. During my last stint on the team, I got the chance to become a team leader. It was a great opportunity to develop my management skills and we got the first position in National Robotic Competition of Indonesia in 2022.

Building Management System

I finished college and then got into the industry to deal with the challenges of control systems. I became an automation engineer and worked on all kinds of projects, like installing sensors and creating user interfaces and data logs. I also built an energy monitoring system to make better use of energy. All my projects were based out of the Sampoerna cigarette factory. I used to work with Schneider and Siemens products. My electrical wiring and logic programming skills were a big help in figuring out these problems.

Undergraduate Thesis

Stability parameters for humanoid robot in its movement can be monitored from the position of COM (Center of Mass) or ZMP (Zero Moment Point). In my undergraduate thesis, I compared between these two parameters. Used IMU sensor to track the COM and then converted it into ZMP and analize its movement.
